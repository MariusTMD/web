FAQ's

Anybody can participate to this online event?

Yes, everybody is allowed to enjoy the show.

Where will be the event live and what I should do before?

The event will be on Zoom and everybody will receive de connection link on email with one day before the event will begins.

Can I have a discount for bigger parties?

Yes, for big events we have a discount of 10%.

Do I have to pay anything upfront?

Yes, we have an upfront fee of 500£, which is non refundable.

I can't select a particular date,time or group size when making an enquiry?

If the date, time slot or group size you are looking for is not selectable from our enquiry form, then I’m afraid we are either fully booked on that date/time or we have no more tables left for your group size.

What happens if I need to cancel a booking? Will I get my deposit back?

If you book with us our cancellation period is 48hrs and we reserve the right to keep the deposit if the booking is cancelled within this time period.

Do you allow under 18's in the venue?

No under 18s are allowed in any of our venues at any time from open-close.
